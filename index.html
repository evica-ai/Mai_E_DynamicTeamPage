Interactive Media Design Student

Fanshawe College

Likes: loves potatoes, marshmallows and pastel aesthetic
Dislikes: watching horror games with popcorn, but not horror movies

Evica Mai strives to become a versatile artist capable of various art and media mediums. Whether it is creating websites, games, print, animation and many other forms of digital media, Evica loves creating a design exuding her style while staying authentic to the message and goal of the task. In fact, she is passionate about combining visual narratives and horror during her idle time, but she also enjoys working on designs and projects that strikes the perfect balance of user experience (functional and accessible) and design. She is ready to take on any challenge with her pink pastel cup of matcha tea. If you would like to contact her, she is available.
